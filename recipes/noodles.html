Noodles

Description

Noodles are more than just strand of dough, they are poetry you can taste. Born from the humble blend of flour, water and salt, they carry centuries of culture in every twist and curl. Whether they dance in streaming bowl of broth and shimmer with oil and spice, noodles tell stories of comfort, of family, of simple joy made sacred. The first bite always feels like home, no matter where you are in the world. There's a softness to the them, a warmth that lingers, wrapping around your soul the way they wrap around your fork or chopsticks.

But what truly makes noodles magical is their adaptability, their willingness to become anything you ask of them. They make the start of a quiet rainy evening or spark of a vibrant feast. They remind us that beauty can come from simplicity that the ordinary, when treated with care , can become extraordinary. Every bowl is an act of devotion, a small, steaming miracle that connects hearts across oceans and generations.

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients to make your delicious noodles at home:

  • Your instant noodles (I recommend indomie)
  • Water
  • Seasoning packets
  • Pepper - very important
  • Your preferred protein
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese, Milk etc. (Noodles are unique, there are different variations to it. It's all up to you)

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add the noodles to the boiling water and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until chewy.
  3. While the noodles cook, you can prepare other ingredients, such as scrambling eggs or chopping vegetables.
  4. Drain the water from the noodles and combine them with the seasoning packet and any other desired ingredients in a bowl or pan.
  5. Mix everything well and serve immediately.
  6. Serve your protein with it the way you would like, noodles are unique for a reason.

Enjoy!
